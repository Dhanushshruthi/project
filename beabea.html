BEBE BLOOM TEA

Bloom sprung from 20 years of passion whilst working with and empowering women to fulfil their goals in life. To deeply connect with their inner wisdom and essence. This includes connecting in a meaningful way to your own cycle and natural rhythms of life and its seasons. When we pause to connect to the natural rhythms of our Mother Earth; it allows us to connect more deeply to our own rhythms and consequently to those around us. Pregnancy, childbirth and parenting are part of these cycles in life, deserving deep holding and support. Bloom a joyful celebration of our artistic, colourful and blissfully unique diversity. Allowing us to celebrate our individuality and that which binds us together as women. Using the wisdom of Western and Celtic Herbal medicine and infused with Oriental and Ayurvedic traditions.
